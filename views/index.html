rmmbr is a tool that lets you send notes to your inbox via SMS. You can use it to send yourself reminders, notes on stuff you want to research later etc.

To use rmmbr, simply text rmmbr <your note> to 41411. This will send you an email with the note as a Google search link. For example, rmmbr send flowers will send you the link: 

http://www.google.com/search?q=send+flowers

To activate rmmbr, text 41411 with the word signup and your email, as in 

rmmbr signup my.email@email.com
Make sure to send this from your own phone. rmmbr will connect the email address in the message with the phone it was sent from.

rmmbr will never knowingly give out your email or phone number. However, it doesn't come with any guarantees - if you use it for critical applications or send sensitive information, you're doing so on your own responsibility.
